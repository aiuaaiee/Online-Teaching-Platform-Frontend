Mission

Contribute to improving high-quality online learning education programs by using technology to promote education anytime, which provides learners with an excellent and comprehensive learning experience. Educators will profit while assisting learners in attaining the necessary information, abilities, and characteristics for professional readiness. To allow everyone to attain higher success in life.

Vision

To be one of the most distinctively quality and accessible education providers online.

Core Values

Excellence

In all our academic endeavors, we aim for greatness. We are dedicated to bringing new ideas to our education, research, and technology. We intend to remain committed to our aim of providing value. We commit to constant self-improvement to attain excellence in all that wedo.

Ethics and Dignity

We are dedicated to upholding the most significant levels of honesty, fairness, respect, professional and intellectual ethics. We expect all our behavior to be grounded in integrity, mutual respect, and decency and guided by the highest ethical standards.

Service

We are committed to serving not just needs of the students and teachers in our virtual communities, as well as the needs of our colleagues.

Diversity

We embrace human diversity in all forms and recognize its importance in maintaining a vibrant learning environment. We are committed to creating a welcoming, friendly, and safe community.

Student Focus

We are dedicated to supporting lifelong learning and leadership development to encourage the professional and personal growth of all learners and educators. These are the primary factors of academic decisions and activity.
